This upload form has been retired for now.
We tried a direct browser-upload button here, but the underlying webhook has a hard ~1 MiB request size limit that most real monthly PDF reports exceed once encoded — so uploads couldn't reliably reach the automation.

Please upload monthly PDF reports to the usual Google Drive folder instead — that path works exactly the same as before and picks up new reports automatically within a few minutes.
